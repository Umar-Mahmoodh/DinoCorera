Who is Dino Corera?

All about me

A multifaceted artist with a passion for advocacy and education, Dino Corera brings a unique flair to his work, characterized by sharp, witty dialogue and innovative thinking that transcends conventional boundaries. His diverse background spans acting, stand-up comedy, voicing, hosting, and radio, accumulating over a decade of experience in theatrical and broadcast realms.

Drawing from his extensive experience in the media industry, Dino now leads 'Layup', an acclaimed startup recognized for its groundbreaking approach to optimizing corporate training engagement. Through inventive and creative methodologies, Layup revolutionizes training and education, earning accolades for its impact and effectiveness.

Additionally, Dino is a prominent figure in the digital sphere, serving as a YouTube personality. Alongside Gehan Blok, he co-founded 'Blok & Dino', a channel renowned for its expertise in content marketing. Through strategic partnerships with some of Sri Lanka's leading brands, Blok & Dino continues to innovate and inspire audiences with engaging and impactful content.

Quintessential media man

With a decade-long journey in media and radio journalism, Dino Corera brings a wealth of experience to his endeavors. Starting from the microphone as a newsreader and radio personality, he swiftly transitioned into management roles, contributing significantly to brand development and digital media conceptualization at the TNL Radio network.

Dino's impact extended beyond his on-air presence; as the host of the morning show and former channel head for 99.2 TNL Radio, he played a pivotal role in shaping the channel's direction. Responsible for strategy, operations, and channel growth, he led with vision and innovation.

Moreover, Dino's influence reached into the digital realm as the head of digital media at TNL Radio network. Here, he played a key role in crafting the digital roadmap for all three stations, leveraging his expertise to propel the network into the digital age.

A creative chameleon

Dino Corera is a familiar face behind the podium, renowned for his captivating presence as a host for a diverse range of brands and corporates. His versatility extends to the stage, where he has enjoyed significant success across various genres, from politically astute stand-up comedy to gripping drama, whimsical theater, and even musical performances, collaborating with esteemed theater companies.

Furthermore, Dino's talent shines through in his role as a stand-up comedian, where he is an integral part of the acclaimed 'Freddy' ensemble. With a distinct reputation for pioneering original local stand-up comedy in Colombo, he continues to captivate audiences with his wit and charm.

Actor & voiceover artist

Dino Corera's acting prowess shines through in an impressive array of roles, showcasing his versatility and depth on stage. Notable credits include portraying 'Marius' in 'Les Miserables' and 'Annas' in 'Jesus Christ Superstar', both presented by the Workshop Players. His talents further extend to productions such as 'Bengal Bungalow' and 'Stormy Weather' by Centrestage Productions, 'And Then There Were None' with Cold Theatre Seven, and 'Glengarry Glen Ross' and 'The Irish Curse' by Identities Inc.

Dino's commitment to the craft is evident in his involvement with various projects aimed at social awareness and advocacy. He has collaborated extensively with the Grassrooted Trust, notably participating in 'V-Day', a project dedicated to promoting gender equality and raising awareness about violence against women.

Internationally, Dino represented Sri Lanka at the prestigious 'Bharat Rang Mahotsav', the second-largest theatre festival globally, as part of the Centrestage Productions ensemble. Here, he showcased his talent in 'Pyramus and Thisby', an adaptation of William Shakespeare's 'Midsummer Night's Dream', further cementing his reputation as a skilled and versatile actor.
