Who is Dino Corera?

All about me

A multifaceted artist with a thirst for awareness and education, Dino’s work carries a penchant for sharp, witty dialogue with an outside-the-box or rather, forget-about-the-box thinking, adding to the brand’s distinct and unique approach to any subject.

Dino Corera is an actor, stand-up comedian, voicing artist, host and former Radio personality with theatrical and broadcast experience spanning over 10 years. With experience in the media backed by a passion for advocacy and awareness, he currently heads 'Layup', an award-winningstartup specializing in optimizing corporate training engagement using novel and creative approaches towards training and education. Dino is also a YouTube personality who together with Gehan Blok co founded 'Blok & Dino', a channel specializing in content marketing which has partnered with some of Sri Lanka's leading brands.

Quintessential media man

Dino is backed by 10 years of experience in media and radio journalism. His career began the mic as a newsreader and radio personality before working behind the scenes in management, brand, and digital media conceptualization at TNL Radio network.

Dino hosts the morning show and is the former channel head for 99.2 TNL Radio and was responsible for strategy, operations and growth for the channel. He was also the head of digital media at TNL Radio network and was instrumental in defining the digital roadmap for all three stations.

A creative chameleon

A familiar presence behind the podium, Dino has hosted for a broad spectrum of brands and corporates on a regular basis. He has also enjoyed considerable success on stage from politically savvy stand-up comedy, serious drama, absurd theater, and a side of musical theater with several established theatre companies

Dino also hosts events for a broad spectrum of brands and corporates on a regular basis and is also a stand up comedian and part of the popular 'Freddy' ensemble with the distinct reputation of introducing original local stand up to audiences in Colombo.

Actor & voiceover artist

As an actor he has portrayed the roles ‘Marius’ in 'Les Miserables' and ‘Annas’ in 'Jesus Christ Superstar' presented by the Workshop Players. Credits further include 'Bengal Bungalow' and 'Stormy Weather' by Centrestage productions, 'And Then There Were None' with Cold Theatre Seven, 'Glengarry Glen Ross' and 'The Irish Curse' presented by Identities Inc. He was cast in multiple roles in the 'Pusswedilla' franchise, Jean Paul Satre’s 'Men Without Shadows' and Duncan McMillan’s critically acclaimed two actor performance 'Lungs' presented by Stage Light and Magic. Dino has worked extensively with the Grassrooted trust with 'V-day' a project that endeavors to shed light and awareness about gender equality and violence against women. He represented Sri Lanka at the second largest theatre festival in the world ‘Bharat Rang Mahotsav’ in India as part of the Centrestage productions ensemble presenting 'Pyramus and Thisby', an adaptation of William Shakespeare’s 'Midsummer Night’s Dream'.

Elevating Digital Presence

Block & Dino

Dino Corera along with his comedy duo Gehan Blok founded Block & Dino, a Sri Lankan digital marketing agency founded by the comedy duo. The agency helps businesses create engaging brand stories and digital experiences.
