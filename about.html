Quintessential media man

Dino is backed by 10 years of experience in media and radio journalism. His career began the mic as a newsreader and radio personality before working behind the scenes in management, brand, and digital media conceptualization at TNL Radio network.

With experience in the media backed by a passion for advocacy and awareness, he currently heads 'Layup', an award-winningstartup specializing in optimizing corporate training engagement using novel and creative approaches towards training and education.

A creative chameleon

A familiar presence behind the podium, Dino has hosted for a broad spectrum of brands and corporates on a regular basis. He has also enjoyed considerable success on stage from politically savvy stand-up comedy, serious drama, absurd theater, and a side of musical theater with several established theatre companies

Along with Gehan, he has worked extensively with the Grass rooted trust on 'V-day', a project that endeavors to shed light and awareness about gender equality and violence against women. He also represented Sri Lanka at the second largest theatre festival in the world ‘Bharat Rang Mahotsav’ in India as part of the Centerstage productions ensemble presenting 'Pyramus and This by', an adaptation of William Shakespeare’s 'Midsummer Night’s Dream'.
