Dino Corera

Who is Dino?

Dino Corera is an actor, stand-up comedian, voicing artist, host and former Radio personality with theatrical and broadcast experience spanning over 10 years. He currently heads 'Layup', an award-winning startup specializing in optimizing corporate training engagement. Dino is also a YouTube personality who together with Gehan Blok co-founded 'Blok & Dino', a channel specializing in content marketing which has partnered with some of Sri Lanka's leading brands.
